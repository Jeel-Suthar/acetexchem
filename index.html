About Acetex

Welcome to About Acetex

Founded in 2015 by experienced chemical professionals, Acetex is built on the foundation of trust, innovation, and sustainable growth. With years of collective expertise, we’ve developed into a globally recognized player in the chemical industry.

Our mission is to enhance industrial performance through superior chemical solutions. We envision a future where our innovation becomes the backbone of multiple industries worldwide.

Our values are simple—quality, commitment, collaboration, and consistency. These guide every decision we make, every product we formulate, and every partnership we build.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure That Drives Precision and Quality

Our success is built on robust infrastructure and world-class facilities.

Every square foot of our infrastructure is built to ensure precision, consistency, and efficiency.

  • State-of-the-Art Production Units with fully automated lines.
  • In-House R&D Lab fostering continuous innovation and customization.
  • Large Storage Capacities for raw materials and finished goods.
  • Well-Connected Logistics to ensure timely delivery across the globe.
  • Quality Control Lab equipped to test every batch before dispatch.
